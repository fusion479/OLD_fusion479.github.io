STUY FUSION 479FIRST Tech Challenge Team 479 from Stuyvesant High School




Stuy Fusion 479 is a part of the Stuyvesant Robotics organization devoted to building and programming robots for the FTC competition.

Over the course of the school year, we construct and program an innovative robot to compete and later demo with, while raising funds and spreading the ideals of FIRST to our community.


Freight Frenzy
Our approach towards designing our robot has remain unchanged for FTC's Freight Frenzy. Our team follows the industry standard model of design thinking, which consists of brainstorming, prototyping, and refining. This process allows us to better understand the mechanisms we build, while possessing the flexibity of an open minded mindset and room for improvement.

Who Are We?
We're FTC Team 479, Stuy Fusion. We are a part of the Stuyvesant Robotics Organization, Stuy Pulse, and have been competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge since 2008.
