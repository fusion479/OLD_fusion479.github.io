STUY FUSION 479FIRST Tech Challenge Team 479 from Stuyvesant High School




Stuy Fusion 479 is a part of the Stuyvesant Robotics organization devoted to building and programming robots for the FTC competition. Over the course of the school year, we construct and program an innovative robot to compete and later demo with, while raising funds and spreading the ideals of FIRST to our community.

The world of transport is undergoing some of the greatest changes of its time, all the while natural disasters, humanitarian crises, and food shortages are happening all around us. Of all the technological advances continuously inserted into our daily lives, which ones will help us transport hard to move goods and solve these global challenges?

Providing basic needs are necessary for cultivating a safe and more equitable world. But doing so can be quite complex. Our world depends on a strong transportation system to get essential items into the hands of those who need it most and move those in danger to safety. When our transportation networks improve with efficiency and scale, our people, our animals, and our livelihoods prevail.

The future of transporting relief to those in need is about inventing creative solutions, pushed by the recognition of the inefficiencies, inequities, and the choices we make around them.

Will we be able to work together and deliver a solution in the race against time? Only time will tell. A better, safer world is possible.
